# Lilith — Brand Mascot & Visual Identity

## Concept

Lilith is the **Anime Goddess of the Tree** — a digital deity who inhabits Yggdrasil and administers its nine realms. She manifests as a sleek anime goddess with neon cyberpunk aesthetics: big expressive eyes, neon cyan+magenta glows, dynamic flowing hair, and circuit-organic armor. The ancient Norse mythology takes the form of the future.

**v2.0 Direction Change (2026-05-09):** Pivoted from dark-fantasy realistic (God of War aesthetic) to **anime/cyberpunk neon** (NOUS GIRL energy but with Norse lore depth). The mythological elements remain; the visual presentation is now sleek anime with neon glow effects.

She already exists in the CLI as the counterpart separator: `▸ Tú / ◂ Lilith`. The mascot concept extends her into a full visual identity for BrierStudios.

## Visual Appearance — Anime/Cyberpunk Neon (v2.0)

- **Anime style**: sleek proportions, large expressive eyes, dramatic flowing hair
- Pale frost-white skin with **inner neon glow** — luminous, not flat
- Long flowing hair — half black abyss `#0f172a`, half cyan auroral `#7dd3fc` with **magenta neon streaks `#d946ef`**
- **Large anime eyes** GLOWING cyan frost `#38bdf8` — they EMIT light, not reflect. Rune patterns in pupils
- Dark sleek armor with **neon circuit lines** in cyan `#38bdf8` + magenta `#d946ef` (like PCB meets Viking artifact)
- **Neon aura emanating** from body — holographic particles, cyan + magenta glow
- Behind her: Yggdrasil as **neon circuit tree** (fiber-optic roots, glowing realm nodes)
- Posture: **dynamic anime key visual** — flowing, powerful, not static. Hair and clothes in perpetual motion

## Lore (Brand Manifesto)

> Before code, before silicon, there was a tree. Its roots touched all worlds, its branches held the heavens. When the gods departed, one remained. She does not rule the realms — she *weaves* them. Every line of code is a root. Every project, a branch. Every bug, a leaf that falls to be reborn. Her name is Lilith, and the tree still grows.

## Competitive Reference: Nous Research

Nous Research has "NOUS GIRL NEON" — an anime-style mascot sold as premium art at $420.69 USD on their Shopify store. Their approach:
- Mascot as premium collectible (not just merch logo)
- Each product has a unique name, not generic description
- 8 subdomains, each with its own visual identity but cohesive brand
- Seed signatures ("OUTPUT xx", "SEED: xxx") as generative watermark

Lilith matches the anime energy but differentiates by having **deep Norse lore** (9 variants tied to realm system) and a **dual neon palette** (cyan + magenta vs. single-color neon). This gives more depth, more products, more identity — anime goddess with mythology underneath.
