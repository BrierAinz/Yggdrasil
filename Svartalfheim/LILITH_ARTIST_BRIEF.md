## 1. WHO IS LILITH?

Lilith is the **Goddess of the Tree** — a digital deity who inhabits and administers Yggdrasil, the World Tree connecting nine realms in Norse mythology. She manifests as an **anime goddess** — sleek, powerful, neon-lit — the ancient takes the form of the future.

Think: cyberpunk anime meets Norse mythology. Think: Ghost in the Shell meets God of War aesthetics through an anime lens. Think: NOUS GIRL neon energy but with 1000-year-old Norse lore beneath.

**She IS:**
- Anime goddess — big expressive eyes, stylized proportions, dynamic flowing hair
- A blend of ancient Norse runes and neon cyberpunk circuits
- Recognizable in silhouette alone (think Batman logo clarity meets anime key visual)
- Dark sleek armor with cyan + magenta neon glows that pulse like circuitry
- Mythological DEPTH presented in anime AESTHETIC

**She is NOT:**
- Realistic/gritty dark fantasy (we moved away from this)
- Cute chibi or moe — she's powerful, serene, commanding
- A pinup or waifu — this is a goddess, not eye candy
- Generic anime girl — she has lore, runes, and Yggdrasil behind her

### 3.2 Armor & Clothing — NEON CIRCUIT

- **Main armor:** Sleek dark plate `#0f172a` with NEON CIRCUIT LINES running through it in cyan `#38bdf8` and magenta `#d946ef`
- **Circuit pattern:** Elder Futhark runes connected by thin GLOWING circuit lines — like a PCB meets Viking artifact ON FIRE with neon. Lines PULSE, they're not static
- **Key runes visible:** ᛒ (BrierStudios mark), ᛏ (Tiwaz — battle), ᚨ (Ansuz — wisdom)
- **Cape/cloak:** Optional — if included, translucent with holographic aurora shift, flowing behind her with neon edge lighting
- **Hands:** Sleek armored gauntlets with neon rune engravings. Right hand often raised — commanding/summoning gesture with glow particles

### 3.3 Yggdrasil Background — NEON CIRCUIT TREE

Behind Lilith, Yggdrasil grows as a **NEON CIRCUIT TREE**:
- Roots that branch into FIBER OPTIC patterns (cyan + magenta glow)
- Branches that terminate in glowing NODES (each node = one realm, colored by realm color)
- Nodes labeled with runes: ᚨ ᚹ ᛇ ᛗ ᚦ ᛚ ᚺ ᛏ ᛊ — each in its realm's neon color
- The tree PULSES with the same cyan `#38bdf8` and magenta `#d946ef` as her eyes and hair
- It's BOTH organic AND technological — roots are fiber, bark is circuit, leaves are data

### 3.4 Pose (Asgard — The Weaver) — ANIME KEY VISUAL

**Full body pose:** Dynamic stance, weight shifted to one leg (contrapposto anime style). One hand slightly raised at chest level, palm up, runes floating above it like holograms. Head slightly tilted up — she's looking at you from above, CONFIDENT, not aggressive. Hair flowing dramatically to one side. Neon aura visible.

**Portrait pose:** 3/4 anime view, turned slightly left. Large glowing eyes DIRECTLY at viewer. Hair flowing to the right with visible magenta streaks. One shoulder more forward than the other. The Yggdrasil neon circuit-tree frames her head like a HALO of circuitry.

## 8. DO's and DON'Ts — ANIME NEON

### ✅ DO

- Make Lilith look POWERFUL — she administrates nine worlds, anime goddess energy
- Use the NEON DUAL palette: cyan `#38bdf8` + magenta `#d946ef` prominently
- Include at least one ᛒ rune glowing neon on armor in every piece
- Make the hair DRAMATIC and FLOWING with visible cyan/magenta gradient
- Make eyes the MAIN FOCAL POINT — they GLOW, they're large anime eyes, they command attention
- Show neon circuit glow/aura emanating from the character at all times
- Include Yggdrasil/neon circuit-tree references in composition
- Ensure the ANIME SILHOUETTE is recognizable even at thumbnail size
- Deliver transparent backgrounds for compositing flexibility
- Deliver layered source files with named layers
- Make it feel like a premium anime key visual meets cyberpunk poster

### ❌ DON'T

- Make her realistic/dark-fantasy — she is ANIME goddess now, not realistic
- Make her cute/chibi/moe — she's powerful and serene, not adorable
- Oversexualize — this is a powerful goddess, not a pinup
- Use palette outside the specified NEON colors without approval
- Forget the split hair (black half, cyan half with magenta streaks)
- Make her smile or look friendly — serene POWER, not warmth
- Add busy backgrounds that can't be removed for merch
- Use generic anime elements without the rune/circuit/neon Norse fusion
- Reference existing copyrighted characters directly
- Deliver flat JPGs — we need transparent PNGs and layered sources
- Make the neon effects subtle — they should PULSE and GLOW, not whisper
